# Quantum Entanglement

Today I learned about the Bell states and how they form the basis of quantum teleportation.

## Key Concepts

- **Superposition**: A qubit can be in state |0>, |1>, or both.
- **Entanglement**: Two qubits can be linked such that the state of one depends on the other.
